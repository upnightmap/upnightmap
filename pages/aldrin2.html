MISSION

Our mission is to map and monitor nighttime safety conditions within UP Diliman. We aim to highlight dark areas and key security points to promote safer mobility on campus. By making this information accessible, we empower the community to move with awareness and confidence.

VISION

We envision a campus where every student can navigate at night without fear or uncertainty. Our goal is to support long-term safety improvements through data-driven insights. Ultimately, we strive for a UP Diliman that is both environmentally and socially secure for all.

Who we are

We are the UP Nightcrawlers , a student group committed to understanding and improving nighttime safety within UP Diliman. This project was created as part of our final requirement for Environmental Science 1 , combining field observation, mapping, and community awareness. Our team collected on-ground data, consolidated it into a night safety map, and built this platform to make our findings useful to fellow students.
